1. GETTING STARTED

1.1 HOW TO LOG IN

Step 1: Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.)

Step 2: Go to the eBakunado login page

Step 3: You will see the Health Worker Portal login form

Step 4: Enter your Mobile Number
   - Format: +63 9XX XXX XXXX
   - Example: +63 912 345 6789

Step 5: Enter your Password
   - Type your password in the password field
   - Click the eye icon to show/hide your password

Step 6: Click the "Login" button

Step 7: If your credentials are correct, you will be redirected to your Dashboard

Note: Make sure you are using the correct mobile number and password assigned to you by your administrator.


1.2 FORGOT PASSWORD

If you forgot your password, follow these steps:

Step 1: On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" link below the password field

Step 2: Enter your Email or Phone Number in the field provided

Step 3: Click "Send OTP" button

Step 4: You will receive an OTP (One-Time Password) via email or SMS

Step 5: Enter the OTP to reset your password

Step 6: Follow the instructions to create a new password

Step 7: Click "Cancel" if you remember your password


1.3 DASHBOARD OVERVIEW

After logging in, you will see your Dashboard. This is your main control center.

The Dashboard shows:
   - Pending Approvals: Number of immunization records waiting for your review
   - Today's Vaccinations: Number of vaccinations scheduled for today
   - Missed Vaccinations: Number of vaccinations that are overdue
   - Total Children: Total number of children in the system

You can click "View Details" on any card to see more information.

The Dashboard also shows:
   - Monthly Vaccine Schedule: Charts showing vaccine distribution for the current and next month
   - You can switch between "Current Month" and "Next Month" views
   - Use the arrow buttons to navigate between months


2. MAIN FEATURES

2.1 DASHBOARD

Location: Left sidebar menu - "Dashboard" (first item)

Purpose: View overview statistics and monthly vaccine schedules

What you can do:
   - See pending approvals count
   - Check today's vaccination schedule
   - View missed vaccinations
   - See total children registered
   - View monthly vaccine charts
   - Navigate to detailed pages by clicking "View Details" on any card

How to use:
   - The dashboard loads automatically when you log in
   - All numbers update automatically
   - Click any "View Details" link to go to the specific page


2.2 VACCINATION PLANNER

Location: Left sidebar menu - "Vaccination Planner"

Purpose: Plan and schedule upcoming vaccinations

What you can do:
   - View vaccination schedule by date
   - Filter by vaccine type, date range, or status
   - See which children need which vaccines
   - Plan vaccination activities in advance
   - Set batch schedule dates for vaccines that require minimum number of babies (e.g., 10 babies to open a vaccine vial)

How to use:

Step 1: Click "Vaccination Planner" from the sidebar

Step 2: Use the filters at the top to narrow down your search

Step 3: Select date range, vaccine type, or status

Step 4: Click "Apply" to load the filtered results

Step 5: View the list of children who need vaccinations

Step 6: Use the table to see detailed information including:
   - Child name
   - Vaccine name and dose number
   - Guideline date (recommended vaccination date)
   - Batch date (scheduled batch vaccination date)
   - Status (scheduled, missed)

SETTING BATCH SCHEDULE DATE

Some vaccines require a minimum number of babies (e.g., 10 babies) to open a vaccine vial. Use the batch schedule feature to group vaccinations together:

Step 1: Select the children who need the same vaccine by checking the boxes in the first column
   - You can select multiple children at once
   - Use the checkbox in the header row to select all children on the current page

Step 2: Click "Set Batch Date" button

Step 3: A popup window will appear asking you to select a date

Step 4: Choose the date when you want to schedule the batch vaccination

Step 5: Click "Confirm" to set the batch date for all selected children

Step 6: The batch date will now appear in the "Batch Date" column for those children

CLEARING BATCH SCHEDULE DATE

If you need to remove a batch schedule date:

Step 1: Select the children whose batch date you want to clear

Step 2: Click "Clear Batch Date" button

Step 3: Confirm the action when prompted

Step 4: The batch date will be removed for the selected children

Note: Setting a batch schedule date helps organize vaccinations when a minimum number of babies is required to open a vaccine vial. This ensures efficient use of vaccine resources.


2.3 IMMUNIZATION FORM

Location: Left sidebar menu - "Immunization Form"

Purpose: Record and manage child immunization records

What you can do:
   - View all immunization records
   - Search for specific children
   - Filter by date, status, or vaccine type
   - Scan QR codes to quickly access child records
   - Add new immunization records
   - Update existing records
   - View missed or upcoming vaccinations

How to use:

Step 1: Click "Immunization Form" from the sidebar

Step 2: Use the search box to find a child by name

Step 3: Use filters to narrow down results:
   - Select date range
   - Choose status (All, Completed, Pending, Missed)
   - Filter by vaccine type

Step 4: Click "Apply" to filter the results

Step 5: Click "Clear" to reset all filters

Step 6: To scan a QR code:
   - Click "Scan QR" button
   - Allow camera permission when prompted
   - Point camera at the child's QR code
   - The system will automatically open the child's record

Step 7: Click on any child's name to view or edit their immunization record

Step 8: To record a vaccination (when a baby actually receives a vaccine):
   - Find the child in the list
   - Click the "Record" button in the Action column
   - A form will open showing the immunization record
   - Fill in the vaccination details:
     * Date the vaccine was given
     * Any additional health information (feeding status, growth measurements, etc.)
     * Mother's Tetanus-Diphtheria (TD) vaccination status if applicable
   - Review all information
   - Click "Submit" or "Save" to record the vaccination
   - The system will update the child's immunization status to "Taken" or "Completed"
   - The schedule will be marked as completed and the record will be saved

Note: The "Record" button is used to document when a baby actually receives a vaccine. This updates the immunization schedule and creates a permanent record of the vaccination.


2.4 PENDING APPROVAL

Location: Left sidebar menu - "Pending Approval"

Purpose: Review and approve immunization records submitted by parents

What you can do:
   - View pending immunization submissions
   - Review child information before approval
   - Approve or reject submissions
   - View added children waiting for family code assignment

How to use:

Step 1: Click "Pending Approval" from the sidebar

Step 2: You will see a list of pending requests

Step 3: Use the search box to find a specific child

Step 4: Use the Status filter to view:
   - "Pending Requests": Immunization records submitted by parents
   - "Added Children": Children you added waiting for approval

Step 5: Review the child's information:
   - Child Name
   - Birth Date
   - Place of Birth
   - Mother's Name
   - Father's Name
   - Address

Step 6: Click "View" button to review the complete child information

Step 7: After reviewing the child's information, you will see two action buttons:
   - "Accept Record" button: Click this to approve the child registration or immunization submission
     * The record will be accepted and added to the system
     * The child will appear in the Child Health Record list
     * Parents will be able to access the child's record
   - "Reject" button: Click this if the information is incorrect or incomplete
     * A confirmation dialog will appear
     * Confirm the rejection
     * The submission will be removed from the pending list
     * Note: This action cannot be undone

Step 8: After accepting or rejecting, you will be returned to the list

Step 9: Click "Back" button to return to the list without making changes

2.7 ADD CHILD

Location: Left sidebar menu - "Add Child"

Purpose: Register a new child in the system

What you can do:
   - Create a new child record
   - Enter child's basic information
   - Enter parent/guardian information
   - Generate a family code for parents to claim the record
   - Set up initial health information

How to use:

Step 1: Click "Add Child" from the sidebar

Step 2: Fill in the "Basic Child Information" section:
   - Child First Name * (required)
   - Child Last Name * (required)
   - Birth Date * (required)
   - Place of Birth
   - Province * (required)
   - City/Municipality * (required)
   - Barangay * (required)
   - Purok
   - Birth Weight (kg)
   - Birth Height (cm)
   - Blood Type
   - Allergies

Step 3: Fill in the "Parent/Guardian Information" section:
   - Mother's First Name * (required)
   - Mother's Last Name * (required)
   - Mother's Mobile Number * (required)
   - Father's First Name
   - Father's Last Name
   - Father's Mobile Number
   - Guardian's Name (if applicable)
   - Guardian's Mobile Number (if applicable)

Step 4: Review all information for accuracy

Step 5: Click "Submit" or "Save" button

Step 6: A family code will be generated

Step 7: Share the family code with the parents so they can claim the child's record in their parent account

Step 8: The child will appear in "Pending Approval" with status "Added Children" until approved

Note: Make sure all required fields (marked with *) are filled correctly. Double-check mobile numbers as parents will use them to claim the record.


2.8 BABY CARD REQUESTS (MIDWIFE ONLY)

Location: Left sidebar menu - "Baby Card Requests" (visible only to Midwives)

Purpose: Manage requests for baby card documents

What you can do:
   - View baby card requests from parents
   - Approve or reject requests
   - Generate baby card PDF documents
   - Track request status

How to use:

Step 1: Click "Baby Card Requests" from the sidebar (Midwife only)

Step 2: View the list of pending baby card requests

Step 3: Each request shows:
   - Parent's name
   - Child's name
   - Request type
   - Date of request

Step 4: For each request, you have two action buttons:
   - "Approve & Generate" button: Click this to approve the request and generate the Baby Card PDF
     * A confirmation dialog will appear
     * Confirm to proceed
     * The system will generate a PDF Baby Card document
     * The request will be marked as approved
     * Parents can download the Baby Card from their account
   - "Reject" button: Click this to reject the request
     * A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you're sure
     * Confirm the rejection
     * The request will be removed from the list
     * Note: This action cannot be undone

Step 5: After approving, the Baby Card PDF is automatically generated and made available to the parent

Note: This feature is only available to Midwives, not regular BHW users.

4. LOGGING OUT

How to Log Out:

Step 1: Click on your profile picture or name in the top right corner

Step 2: A dropdown menu will appear

Step 3: Scroll down to the bottom of the menu

Step 4: Click "Logout" button (it may be in red/danger color)

Step 5: You will be automatically logged out and redirected to the login page

Note: Always log out when you finish your work, especially if you are using a shared computer. This protects your account and patient information.


5. TIPS & BEST PRACTICES

1. NAVIGATION TIPS
   - Use the left sidebar menu to navigate between pages
   - The active page is highlighted in the sidebar
   - Click the menu icon in the top left to show/hide the sidebar

2. SEARCHING TIPS
   - Use the search boxes to quickly find children or records
   - Search works with partial names (you don't need to type the full name)
   - Use filters to narrow down large lists

3. QR CODE SCANNING TIPS
   - Make sure you allow camera permission when prompted
   - Ensure good lighting when scanning
   - Hold the camera steady about 10-15 cm away from the QR code
   - The scanner will automatically open the child's record when successful

4. DATA ENTRY TIPS
   - Always double-check information before submitting
   - Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*)
   - Make sure mobile numbers are correct (parents use them to claim records)
   - Save your work frequently when filling long forms

5. SECURITY TIPS
   - Never share your login credentials with anyone
   - Always log out when finished
   - Use strong passwords
   - Report any suspicious activity to your administrator

6. TROUBLESHOOTING
   - If a page doesn't load, try refreshing the page (F5 or refresh button)
   - If you can't log in, check your mobile number and password
   - If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" feature
   - Clear your browser cache if you experience display issues
   - Contact your system administrator for technical support


6. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What should I do if I can't log in?
A: Check your mobile number and password. Use "Forgot Password" if needed. Contact your administrator if the problem persists.

Q: How do I find a specific child's record?
A: Use the search box on any list page, or scan their QR code if available.

Q: What's the difference between "Pending Requests" and "Added Children"?
A: "Pending Requests" are submissions from parents. "Added Children" are children you added that are waiting for approval.

Q: How do I generate a family code?
A: When you add a new child, a family code is automatically generated. Share this code with the parents so they can claim the record.

Q: Can I edit a child's information after adding them?
A: Yes, go to "Child Health Record", find the child, and click on their name to view and edit their information.

Q: What should I do if I make a mistake?
A: You can usually edit records. If you can't edit something, contact your administrator for help.

Q: How often should I check pending approvals?
A: Check regularly, especially daily, to ensure timely processing of immunization records.

Q: What if the QR scanner doesn't work?
A: Make sure you allowed camera permission. Try refreshing the page. Ensure good lighting and hold the camera steady.
