About Our System

The eBakunado is a digital solution developed for Linao Health Center to simplify immunization record-keeping. It helps health workers and parents manage vaccine schedules, track upcoming doses, and maintain accurate records. With this system, the traditional manual process of writing and storing immunization data in logbooks is replaced with a faster, safer, and more organized approach. Parents can easily view their child’s vaccination history, receive reminders for upcoming immunizations, and ensure that no dose is missed.

For health workers, eBakunado reduces the chances of data loss, human error, and the difficulty of retrieving old records by providing a centralized and searchable digital database. It also supports better planning of vaccination programs since staff can monitor which vaccines are due, which children are overdue, and generate reports with ease.

By combining technology with healthcare, eBakunado not only improves efficiency at the Linao Health Center but also strengthens the community’s trust in the immunization process. It is designed to be user-friendly, reliable, and secure, making immunization management accessible both for parents and healthcare providers.
